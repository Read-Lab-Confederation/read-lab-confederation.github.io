The strain of defining the bacterial strain

Tim Read

Introduction: A strain by any other name … Large-scale sequencing is giving us a clearer picture of what bacterial species are - clusters of core genomes with more similarities to each other than other species’ clusters. Horizontal gene transfer can make the boundaries more or less fuzzy but using metrics such as average nucleotide identity 1,2, in the majority of cases, we can clearly see when two genomes are not in the same species.
